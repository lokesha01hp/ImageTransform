How to Use ImageTransForm

  1. Click on Choose File and select your image (JPG, PNG, WEBP, etc.).
  2. Select the desired format you wish to convert your image to (PNG, JPG, GIF, etc.).
  3. Click on the Submit button to upload and process your image.
  4. A success message with a Download link will appear after processing.
  5. Click on the download link to save your converted image directly to your device.
